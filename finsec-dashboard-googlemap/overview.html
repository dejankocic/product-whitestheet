KPI Explainers
  • Risk Index: Composite score (0–100) combining severity, frequency and recency of threats. Lower is better; values above 70 warrant attention.
  • Cost Avoided: Estimated loss prevented by blocks/auto-resolutions, modeled from incident type × likelihood × benchmark impact. It’s directional, not accounting-grade.
  • Risk Score Trend: Change of risk index over time; rising trend indicates deteriorating posture or targeted campaigns.
